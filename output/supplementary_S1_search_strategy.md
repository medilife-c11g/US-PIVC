## General Search Parameters
- **Date range**: 1 January 2000 to 27 March 2026
- **Language restriction**: English
- **Search date**: 28-29 March 2026

The search strategy combined three conceptual domains:
1. **Ultrasound guidance** (MeSH/Emtree terms + free text)
2. **Peripheral intravenous catheterisation** (MeSH/Emtree terms + free text)
3. **Post-insertion outcomes** (MeSH/Emtree terms + free text)

## Deduplication Method
Three-layer deduplication was performed using Python:
1. **DOI matching** (exact)
2. **PMID matching** (exact)
3. **Title similarity** (Jaccard coefficient >= 0.85)
