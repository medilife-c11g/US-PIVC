## Headline results

- Pooled catheter failure: RR 1.23 (0.99–1.51), p = 0.056 — borderline non-significant
  - REML+HKSJ post-hoc sensitivity: RR 1.225 (1.005–1.493), p = 0.047
  - **Cohort-only**: RR 1.29 (1.02–1.63), p = 0.036
  - **RCT-only**: RR 1.03 (0.66–1.61), p = 0.88
  - Saltarelli 2015 (49.8% weight) excluded → RR 1.18 (0.88–1.58), p = 0.28
- Dwell time: not pooled (I²=91.9%); narrative synthesis only
- Extravasation: not pooled (I²=95.7%); narrative synthesis only — possible safety signal flagged
- Infiltration: RR 0.68 (0.12–3.83), no difference
- HR-based failure: HR 1.02 (0.75–1.38), no difference
